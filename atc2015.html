Art & Technology Conference 2015

Creative Director

"Art&Technology Conference" is the biggest annual event in Ar&Technology Department, Sogang University, Seoul, Korea. HyeLeeKim directed the 4th ATC 2015.

The 4th Art&Technology Conference from November 30th to December 1st in 2015 consisted of two parts: Thematic exhibition and free exhibition. 23 artworks in Digital design, interactive art, game, media installation, sound art, hybrid art, virtual reality, performance genre were entered to the thematic exhibition and 8 works for free exhibition were entered and exhibited in 2015 ATC. Through 6 months, artists completed their works in cooperation with the curators considering about the 'uncertainty' in their lives under the topic of 'Mondy Morning, the question started over.'

During the exhibition period, the workshop 'Making my own 3D Hologram' for the public, and the debate between the curator, critics, artists and the student panel (topic: "The necessity of communication in arts") and artists' talk also had been in progress together.
